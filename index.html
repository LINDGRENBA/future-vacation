My Dream Vacation:

Wales

I'd like to visit Wales some day because my Nanny (that's what we call my grandmother) was originally from Wales and I still have family there who run a Bed and Breakfast. All of the pictures I've seen of Wales look quite beautiful and I'd be interested to visit the place where my Nanny grew up.

Vacation Packages

The Romantic Package

  • Cost: $4000
  • Available Dates: February 14th - 21st
  • Activities:
    • Spend three days at the Llandudno beach resort.
    • Sample wines at Ancre Hill Estates.
    • Treat yo' self at the St Brides Hotel & Spa.

The Outdoors Package

  • Cost: $2000
  • Available Dates: March - November
  • Activities:
    • Hike the beautiful Snowdonia.
    • Visit Brecon Beacons National Park.
    • Observe dolphins and other wild life on a boat tour.

The Family Fun Package

  • Cost: $5000
  • Available Dates: Various start dates available year round.
  • Activities:
    • Visit the famousAnimal Wall at Cardiff Castle.
    • Spend three days at the Llandudno beach resort.
    • Learn to rock climb at the Beacon Climbing Centre in Caernafon.

The Foodie Package

  • Cost: $4500
  • Available Dates: Various start dates available year round.
  • Activities:
    • Eat Welsh Rarebit in Cardiff.
    • Visit the Swansea Market to try local treats.
    • Visit Caws Teifi to sample award-winning cheeses.
